Vision & Mission

Vision

Sardauna Academy an educational institution designed to provide learning spaces and learning enviroments for the teaching of students under the direction of teachers. We plan for quality higher education programmes with complete progression pathway for your childrens/wards. Our distinguished Academic and Examination Board also sets the academic and examination standards of Sardauna Academy programmes. We make learning exclusive for you. We know everyone has different learning needs and lifestyles. We have learning modes ranging from traditional classroom learning, to e-learning and a combination of both to accommodate different learning patterns. We strive to provide learning and support services, as well as a conducive environment for you to learn, live and develop into global thought leaders of tomorrow. We focus in giving you the best.

About the School

SARDAUNA ACADEMY (SA) is one among the leading and oldest school. The school is reputed for its excellence in teaching and training engineering students for generations since 2000. The school caters Nursery, Primary and Secondary. courses in all the specialised areas. The school of SA has pioneered in setting education standards par excellence. Endowed with futuristic vision of generating sustained quality manpower for the ever-increasing global demand of Education, the school of SA is well equipped. 

We have 30 qualified and trained teachers in our school. 

With the widespread amount of illitracy in all the fields, the demand for the Education is on a high note and provides a very good job opportunity in various domains. The students can also pursue higher studies and research Nigeria. The students can also become entrepreneurs without any major investment. There is a lot of scope for freelancers and they can earn huge amount of money.
